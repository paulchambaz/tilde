Tilde replaces the standard Android launcher with three screens navigated by vertical swipe. The home screen shows the time, date, and a short list of labeled shortcuts to your most-used apps. Swipe down to open the app drawer, which filters your installed apps as you type. Swipe up to expand the notification shade.

From the home screen, tapping the time, tapping the date, swiping left, and swiping right each launch a configurable app. The settings screen is accessible from the app drawer.

There are no icons, no widgets, and no wallpapers.
